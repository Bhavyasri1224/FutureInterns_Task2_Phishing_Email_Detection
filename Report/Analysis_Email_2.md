# Email Sample 2 Analysis

## Email Subject
Bank Account Verification Required

## Indicators Found

- Generic greeting ("Dear Customer")
- Urgent language ("Account suspended")
- Suspicious banking URL
- Requests account verification
- Unknown sender domain

## Risk Classification

PHISHING

## Explanation

This email attempts to trick users into believing their bank account has been suspended. It uses urgency and a suspicious link to steal sensitive information such as login credentials.
