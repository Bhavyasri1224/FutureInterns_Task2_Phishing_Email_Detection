# Email Sample 3 Analysis

## Email Subject
Congratulations! You Won a Gift Card

## Indicators Found

- Unsolicited reward offer
- Suspicious URL
- Urgent message ("Offer expires today")
- Unknown sender

## Risk Classification

PHISHING

## Explanation

This email uses a fake reward offer to attract victims. The urgency and suspicious website indicate an attempt to steal personal information or redirect users to malicious content.
