# Email Sample 1 Analysis

## Email Subject
Urgent: Your Account Will Be Locked

## Indicators Found

- Generic greeting ("Dear User")
- Urgent language ("Account will be locked")
- Suspicious URL
- Unknown sender domain

## Risk Classification

PHISHING

## Explanation

This email uses fear and urgency to pressure the user into clicking a suspicious link. The sender domain and URL are not associated with a trusted organization, indicating a phishing attempt.
